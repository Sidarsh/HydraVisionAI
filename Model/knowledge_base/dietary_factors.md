# Dietary Factors

Diet plays a crucial role in skin hydration from the inside out.
- Water Intake: Consuming at least 8 glasses (2 liters) of water daily is essential.
- Omega-3 Fatty Acids: Found in fish, walnuts, and flaxseeds, these help strengthen the skin's lipid barrier.
- Antioxidants: Vitamins C and E help protect the skin from oxidative stress which can damage the barrier.
- Avoid Alcohol and Caffeine in excess, as they can act as diuretics and lead to dehydration.
