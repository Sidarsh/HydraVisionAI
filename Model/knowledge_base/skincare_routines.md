# Skincare Routines

For dehydrated skin, a gentle routine is recommended:
1. Cleansing: Use a mild, non-foaming cleanser to avoid stripping natural oils.
2. Toning: Avoid alcohol-based toners. Use hydrating toners containing glycerin or hyaluronic acid.
3. Serums: Apply hyaluronic acid on damp skin to draw in moisture.
4. Moisturizing: Use occlusives or ceramides to lock in the hydration and repair the skin barrier. (Examples in India: Minimalist Ceramide Cream, Dot & Key Barrier Repair).
5. Sun Protection: Always apply broad-spectrum sunscreen.
